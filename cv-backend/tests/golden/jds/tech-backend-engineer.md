Senior Backend Engineer

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team.
You will design and build the services that power our SaaS product,
serving thousands of B2B customers globally.

Responsibilities:
- Design and ship microservices in Python, backed by PostgreSQL.
- Deploy and operate workloads on AWS using Docker and Kubernetes.
- Build and maintain REST APIs consumed by our web and mobile clients.
- Own ci/cd pipelines for your services and improve build/deploy
  feedback loops.

About you:
- Strong problem solving skills and a track record of shipping
  production systems.
- Excellent communication skills and comfort collaborating with
  cross-functional teams (product, design, data).
- You take ownership of what you ship — from prototype to on-call.
- Experience working in an agile environment.

Nice to have:
- Experience with cloud platforms beyond AWS.
- Familiarity with observability tooling.
