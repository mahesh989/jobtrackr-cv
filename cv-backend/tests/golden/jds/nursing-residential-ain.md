Assistant in Nursing — Residential Aged Care

Our residential aged care home is seeking a committed Assistant in
Nursing (AIN) to join our team. You will deliver personal care to our
residents in a busy 60-bed home.

Key responsibilities:
- Support residents with showering, dressing and grooming as part of
  daily personal care.
- Assist residents with mobility, including hoist transfers and safe
  manual handling.
- Provide medication assistance under the supervision of the RN, and
  contribute to clinical documentation at the end of each shift.
- Build trusting relationships with residents and their families.

About you:
- You can show commitment to allocated shifts across weekdays, evenings
  and weekends.
- You work well as part of a team and have effective verbal and written
  communication skills.
- You bring empathy and a person-centred approach to every interaction.

Qualifications:
- Certificate III in Individual Support (Ageing) or equivalent.
- NDIS Worker Check (or willingness to apply).
- Current First Aid and CPR.
