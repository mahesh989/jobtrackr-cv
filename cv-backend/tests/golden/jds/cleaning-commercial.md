Commercial Cleaner — Office Buildings

We are seeking experienced commercial cleaners for our portfolio of
office buildings across the CBD. This is an afternoon/evening shift
role, Monday to Friday.

What you will be doing:
- Routine commercial cleaning of office floors, including vacuuming
  carpeted areas and mopping hard floors.
- Bathroom cleaning, restocking supplies and waste management across
  the building.
- Wiping down surfaces, dusting, and emptying bins.
- Safe use of cleaning chemicals and PPE at all times.

What we are looking for:
- Reliability for scheduled shifts and good timekeeping.
- High attention to detail — our clients notice everything.
- Ability to work independently without close supervision.
- Follows site safety procedures and instructions.

Required:
- Police check.
- Right to work in Australia.
