Personal Care Worker — Home Care Services

We are recruiting Personal Care Workers to deliver in-home care to
older people across the Western Sydney region. The role focuses on
helping clients remain independent in their own homes.

What you will do:
- Provide personal care in clients' homes, including showering, dressing
  and toileting assistance.
- Support clients with light domestic assistance — laundry, meal
  preparation and general household tasks.
- Assist with mobility around the home and on community outings.
- Build strong, trusting relationships with clients and their families
  over months and years of regular visits.

We are looking for someone who:
- Has reliability for allocated shifts — our clients depend on consistent
  carers.
- Demonstrates empathy and a warm, respectful manner.
- Has effective verbal communication skills and can write clear progress
  notes after each visit.

Requirements:
- Driver's licence and reliable car.
- Police check.
- Certificate III in Individual Support or equivalent.
